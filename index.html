About Me

I'm urban and minimalist photographer! I shoot in black and white as it allows me to focus on the composition and contrast of my images. This classic style adds a timeless quality to my photographs and can evoke a sense of nostalgia or drama. Black and white photography can be a powerful tool for telling stories and creating a unique visual experience.

I like Urbanistic stuff

As a minimalist urban photographer, my goal is to capture the soul of a cityscape in its purest and most essential form. I strive to create images that are both simple and powerful, using clean lines, geometric shapes, and strong contrasts to convey a sense of tranquility and calmness.

Abstract is a passion

There is something truly magical about capturing the essence of a scene or an object in a way that is completely unique and unexpected. I find that the abstract approach allows me to tap into my creativity in a way that other forms of photography do not, and it challenges me to constantly push the boundaries of what is possible.

I'm also a Visual Artist

As a visual artist, I am constantly exploring new ways to express myself and connect with the world around me. I am fascinated by the power of images to communicate complex ideas and emotions, and I believe that art has the ability to transcend language and cultural barriers.

I love Nature

Taking photographs of nature is a meditative and peaceful experience. It allows me to connect with the environment on a deeper level and appreciate the beauty that surrounds us. I feel grateful to be able to capture these moments and share them with others, and I hope that my photographs can inspire others to appreciate the beauty of the natural world as well.

Contact Me

If you're interested in my work or have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact me. I'm always happy to hear from fellow artists, potential clients, or anyone who shares my passion for minimalism.
